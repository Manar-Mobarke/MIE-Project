About Us.

Made In Egypt

It was founded in 2006, where students from all over the Egyptian engineering universities compete with their peers with their graduation projects. The target is to bring the best out of them so they are able and ready to convert their graduation projects into their startups by connecting them with the business approaches besides the technical ones.

Mission

To be the leading force in fostering innovation within engineering 'Made in Egypt' aims to inspire and equip a generation of senior students with the creativity and excellence to exceed market expectations
