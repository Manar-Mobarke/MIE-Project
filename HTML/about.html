About Us.

Made In Egypt

It was founded in 2006, where students from all over the Egyptian engineering universities compete with their peers with their graduation projects. The target is to bring the best out of them so they are able and ready to convert their graduation projects into their startups by connecting them with the business approaches besides the technical ones.

Mission

To be the leading force in fostering innovation within engineering 'Made in Egypt' aims to inspire and equip a generation of senior students with the creativity and excellence to exceed market expectations

IEEE

IEEE is the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. It's a non-profit organization Built by a group of engineers who recognized the importance of spreading knowledge and technology in the world

IEEE Young Professionals

IEEE is an IEEE affinity group that encourages the participation of young engineers in promoting the engineering community, fosters real communication between the university and the industry, and enhances the skills needed for better performance in the professional world. YP started in Egypt in 2002, and thanks to its fast and confident growth, it is now one of the most active affinity groups in IEEE Region 8, which includes Europe, Africa, and Middle East.
